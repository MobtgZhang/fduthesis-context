\startabstracten

This document serves as the user manual for the Fudan University thesis \ConTeXt\ template
(fduthesis-context). The template strictly follows the
{\em Fudan University Doctoral and Master's Thesis Regulations (October 2024 Revision)}
and supports bachelor's, master's, and doctoral thesis types,
as well as academic and professional degree categories.

This document covers installation, configuration, text formatting,
figures and tables, mathematical formulas, bibliography management,
and other features of the template.
The document itself demonstrates the typesetting quality of the template.
